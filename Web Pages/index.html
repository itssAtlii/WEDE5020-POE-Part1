About Us

Derma Glow,founded in the year 2024,is a small online store that sells organic skincare products and plans to provide smart skincare services online. Our product is a sustainability focused product made with 97% synthetic free ingredients. The main ingredients used in the manufacturing of the products include,the African Shea Tree Butter,Aloe Vera,essential oils,vitamins and many more. Derma Glow's aim in the self-care and beauty space is to cater to all skin types by providing healthy and quality products and giving consumers a personal and healthy well-being experience. Our vision as a brand is to fuel people's desire for self-care and personal expression through personalized skincare routines, paired with our affordable luxury products,whilst putting sustainability at the forefront.
